Founder / studio photo

Started at a kitchen table, not a boardroom.

Vaani Straps began as a small side project — a handful of earrings made for friends, sold out of a backpack at weekend markets. Every piece since has kept that same standard: would I actually wear this every day?

"I wanted accessories that felt like they belonged to you after the first wear, not the tenth."

Today the collection has grown to include bags and a few other essentials, but the process hasn't changed — small batches, real materials, and pieces built to be used.
